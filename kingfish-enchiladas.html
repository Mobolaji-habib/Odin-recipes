Kingfish Enchiladas

kingfish,aka King Mackeral, is a strong flavored sport fish which pairs well with zesty flavor of salsa.Make sure to remove

remove all the dark meat from the fish when you clean it.

ingredients

  • 1 pound king mackerel fillets-cleaned,washed and
  • 1/2 cup italian-style salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 onion,diced
  • 1 green bell pepper,diced
  • 1(16 ounce) jar salsa

Directions

step 1

combine fish cubes with italian dressing.Let marinate for at least 30 minutes.Dredge

fish cubes in flour and set aside

Step 2

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).Lightly oil a 9x13 inch baking pan.

Step 3

In a non-stick frying pan,heat olive oil over medium high heat.Add onions and green

pepper. Cook slowly, stirring occassionally until vegetables are soft.Stir in fish cubes

and cook until fish is soft,about 5 minutes.Stir in half of the salsa and remove

mixture from heat.

Step 4

Spoon mixture into tortillas,roll up and place seam down in prepared baking pan.

Spoon remaining salsa over the enchiladas and sprinkles with shredded cheese.

Step 5

Bake in preheated oven for fifteen
