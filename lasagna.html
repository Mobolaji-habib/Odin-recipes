Enchilada Lasagna

This enchilada lasagna is perfect for dinner or potlucks

ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion,chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic,chopped
  • 1 pounds ground turkey
  • 1(28 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  • i(14.5 ounce) package small-curd cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 table spoon ground cumin
  • 5(6 inch) corn tortillas,halved
  • 2 cups shredded mexican cheese blend
  • cooking spray
  • 1 green onion,diced

    5. Mix cottage cheese,egg, and cumin together in a small bowl.

  5. Step 5

    the Mexican cheese on top Repeat layers once more,ending with remaining turkey

    sauce and Mexican cheese

  6. Step 6

    7. Spray a large piece of aluminum foil with cooking spray ; cover the baking dish with

    8. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes. Remove aluminum foil
